A trait for maps from K to V and implementations.

The Lookup<K,V> trait corresponds to a map from K to V. This can be implemented many different ways based on different requirements. A HashMap<K,V> is a general solution for K: Hash, whereas a Vec<(K,V)> is a primitive but often effective solution for K: Eq, the minimal requirement.

Other implementations include a Vec<Option<V>> for usize keys, and a RHHMap implementation based on a Robin Hood hashing implementation.
